Facilities Maintenance Team (FMT)
Narrator
Shortly after submitting the report, you are contacted by the Facilities Maintenance Team (FMT) regarding the issues affecting your office.

The team responds promptly, arranging a site inspection to assess the air-conditioning fault and water supply disruption.

After identifying the root causes, repairs are carried out efficiently, restoring the facilities to full operational condition.

With everything functioning smoothly again, you are able to maintain productivity and focus on growing the business — confident that dependable operational support is always within reach.

Over time, this stability allows your business to mature, expand its network, and explore opportunities beyond local operations.
