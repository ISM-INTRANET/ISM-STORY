Industry Cluster Development (ICD)
Narrator
You meet with the Industry Cluster Development (ICD) team to discuss the next phase of your company’s growth.

During the discussion, you share your plans to bring Amajon, a major international ICT company from Malaysia, into Brunei through a joint venture.

You explain the key requirements — suitable land and office space to support operations, collaboration, and future expansion.

Taking this into consideration, the ICD team begins to facilitate the entry of Amajon into Brunei’s ICT ecosystem.

Recognising the importance of clustering, ICD proposes placing Amajon near your existing office at Anggerek Desa Technology Park (ADTP), within the established ICT Cluster, to encourage collaboration, talent sharing, and innovation.
