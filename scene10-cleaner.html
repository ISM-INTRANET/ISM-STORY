A Helpful Hint
Narrator
You approach the cleaner working nearby and explain the issues you are experiencing in your office.

The cleaner listens politely but looks slightly confused. He explains that handling air-conditioning and water issues is not part of his role.

Before you leave, he mentions that there is someone from ISM who usually handles maintenance-related matters in the building, and suggests that you check with them instead.

With this hint in mind, you reconsider who the right person to approach might be.
