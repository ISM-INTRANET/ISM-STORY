BEDB Tenant Journey
This simulation places you in the role of a business owner navigating the journey of becoming a BEDB Tenant.

Through a series of scenarios and decisions, you will explore how teams within the ISM Department at BEDB support businesses — from initial interest and applications, through tenancy and ongoing operations.

Your choices help illustrate the roles, responsibilities, and impact of each step in the BEDB Tenant Journey.
