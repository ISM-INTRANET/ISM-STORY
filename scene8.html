A New Beginning
Narrator
After completing the required steps and formalities, you receive confirmation that marks a key milestone in your journey.

You are officially recognised as a Tenant under the Brunei Economic Development Board, opening the door to a new phase of growth and opportunity.

As part of this milestone, you are allocated an office space within the Anggerek Desa Technology Park (ADTP), providing a foundation to begin operations and build your business further.

What began as an idea has now taken shape — a structured start within the BEDB Ecosystem.

As you look ahead, it is clear that this is only the beginning.
