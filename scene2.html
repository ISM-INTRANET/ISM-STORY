BEDB - ISM
Narrator
While researching potential solutions, you come across the Brunei Economic Development Board (BEDB) .

You discover that BEDB offers various spaces tailored for businesses — whether it’s industrial land for long-term development, or a ready-built factory for faster setup and immediate operations.

More importantly, you learn that under BEDB, there is a dedicated department called Industrial Site Management (ISM) — a team that assists tenants with site matters, facilities, and operational challenges as their businesses grow.

With a clearer path ahead, you decide to visit the BEDB office the next day, ready to learn more about the support available and what it could mean for your business.
