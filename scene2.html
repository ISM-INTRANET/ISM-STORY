Scene 2 — Discovering Opportunities
Narrator
While researching potential solutions, Awi comes across the Brunei Economic Development Board (BEDB).

He discovers that BEDB offers various spaces tailored for businesses — whether it’s industrial land for long-term development, or a ready-built factory for faster setup and immediate operations.

More importantly, Awi learns that under BEDB, there is a dedicated department called Industrial Site Management (ISM) — a team that assists tenants with site matters, facilities, and operational challenges as their businesses grow.

With a clearer path ahead, Awi decides to drop by the BEDB office tomorrow to meet an ISM representative and learn more about the support available.
