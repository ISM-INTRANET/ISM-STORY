At the Wrong Desk
Narrator
You are greeted by an officer who listens carefully as you explain your interest in securing a space for your business.

As the discussion unfolds, it becomes clear that this team focuses on tenant matters, rather than handling business applications and assessments.

The officer politely explains that applications are managed by a different team and points you toward the right starting point.

With this clarification, you step back into the lobby—now better informed and ready to approach the correct team.
