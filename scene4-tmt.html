At the Wrong Desk
Narrator
You are politely greeted, but it quickly becomes clear that this may not be the right place to start.

As the conversation continues, you realise that your visit is not related to an application or assessment process — which is what you are actually here for.

With a brief clarification, you are advised to speak with a different team that handles business applications and evaluations.

You thank them for the guidance and prepare to head back, now with a better idea of where to go next.
