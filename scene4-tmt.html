Understanding the Process
Narrator
You are greeted by an officer who listens carefully as you explain your interest in securing a space for your business.

As the discussion continues, the officer takes time to walk you through the process and how different teams support businesses at different stages. The Tenant Management Team (TMT) focuses on tenant-related matters, ensuring businesses receive the right support once they are in place.

To help move your enquiry forward, the officer explains that the initial application and allocation process is handled separately, and prepares you for the next step in your journey.

With a clearer understanding of the process, you’re now better equipped to decide who to speak with next.
