Business Assessment and Allocation (BAA)
Narrator
MC is welcomed by the Business Assessment and Allocation (BAA) team at BEDB.

The team patiently walks him through the application process, helping him prepare the necessary documents and clarify the requirements based on his business needs.

His proposal then goes through a screening and assessment stage, where the BAA team evaluates the viability of his business plan, operational readiness, and site requirements.

With guidance provided at every step, MC gains a clearer understanding of what is expected — and how to strengthen his application.

After the assessment, the BAA team assists in the allocation process, identifying a suitable site that aligns with his business objectives and growth plans.

What once felt overwhelming now feels structured, supported, and achievable.
