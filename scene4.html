Business Assessment and Allocation (BAA)
Narrator
You are welcomed by the Business Assessment and Allocation (BAA) team at BEDB.

They guide you through the application process step by step, helping you prepare the necessary documents and clarifying requirements based on your business needs.

Your proposal then moves into the screening and assessment stage, where your business plan, operational readiness, and site requirements are carefully reviewed.

Along the way, you gain a clearer understanding of what is expected — and how your application can be strengthened to meet those expectations.

Once the assessment is complete, the BAA team supports the allocation process, identifying a site that aligns with your business objectives and future growth plans.

What initially felt complex and uncertain now feels structured, supported, and within reach.
